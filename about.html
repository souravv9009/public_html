    About Us

    AROVA EXPORTS PRIVATE LIMITED started in the year 2019 at Gangavathi(Karnataka,India).We export Spices, Fresh fruits, vegetables, etc. Under the leadership of Mr.Muqeeb pasha B, Managing Director of the company , the company has emerged as one amoung the leading exporters from India.

    Arova Exports is a customer-oriented company, established for the objective of serving before earning. Our single most important objective isto provide a service that our clients remember - We're not looking for one-timecustomers but relationships that could go on for ages. A team of humble andhonest individuals, we're aiming towards boosting the exports of our nation bysupplying quality products to the world beyond ours.

    It's our belief that customer satisfaction is the real wealth.Importer's consent towards our products is important and valuable for us. The most valuable thing for us is not the margin that we keep or the investment that we make into our business, but the clients whom we touch. Arova Exports is founded on the principle of "Faith and Honesty" - And we shallforever let our positive belief system drive our business.
